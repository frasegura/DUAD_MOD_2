Social Networks

Social networks are websites and apps that allow users and organizations to connect, communicate, share information and form relationships.
People can connect with others in the same area, families, friends, and those with the same interests.

Conclusions

Social media has many advantages, including improved communication, business opportunities, and instructional materials.
Navigating its difficulties, such as privacy issues, false information, and effects on mental health, is vital.
